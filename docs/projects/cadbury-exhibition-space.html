CADBURY
EXHIBITION SPACE

An exhibition space that brings the Cadbury brand to life by creating an emotional connection with the visitors by stimulating their senses by putting them at the centre of the experience. Triggering the joy reflex by connecting Cadbury to happiness.

01

CHALLENGE

To design an exhibition space for the brand Cadbury. The space had to reflect brand values and promote brand identity and awareness. It was important to create an environment that builds and maintain a personal & emotional connection with visitors so that they can have a memorable brand experience.
