PRODUCT DESIGN

GATSBY OTTOMAN

Inspired by the opulence of the Roaring '20s, the Gatsby Ottoman is a statement piece that speaks style and class.

01

CHALLENGE

To design an ottoman for a residential space that would stand out as a statement piece.

Year: 2015 Project Type: Residential Furniture Design Team members: Design Team of Josmo Studio in Mumbai About Josmo Studio: Born in the heart of contemporary Mumbai, Josmo Studio is immersed in revolutionizing expression through design. Josmo believes in essence, the magic lies within transforming radical ideas into purposeful furniture. Pieces custom made to fit together, but more importantly, to fit you. Blending Craft and Industry to create pieces that, in fact, speak to you. The following design were made during my time as a Junior Designer at Josmo Studio, under the guidance of Anjali Mody.
02

PROCESS

Benchmarking and design research on other ottoman designs was carried out. Various ideas were conceptualised by experimenting with different forms, seating style and material combination. A concept was finalised which was then further detailed out by designing the finer elements and choosing the appropriate materials.

My role: Design research, conceptualisation, sketching of final ideas & detailing.
03

OUTCOME

The Gatsby Ottoman has a rich indigo hue that adds to the majesty of the piece while the hand-crafted brass accents enhance the vintage feel. The velvet used is of an extremely high quality and reflects the luxury and glamour of the 1920s. Materials: Upholstered fabric, wooden frame, brass frame detailing, clear matte lacquer, teak stained with clear PU matte finish.
