PRODUCT DESIGN

HOT 'N' COLD

Inspired by the beautiful hourglass form, this all-in-one teapot design makes both hot & iced cold tea at the same time and in the same pot. Designed for sight, smell and touch, this interactive tea-making experience upgrades your convenience of making and drinking tea.
